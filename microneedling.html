What is Microneedling?

Microneedling, also known as collagen induction therapy, is a minimally invasive procedure that uses fine, medical-grade needles to create micro-injuries on the skin. These microchannels stimulate the body’s natural healing process, boosting collagen and elastin production, which results in firmer, smoother, and healthier-looking skin.

Benefits of Microneedling

  • Reduces acne scars
  • Fades pigmentation and dark spots
  • Improves skin texture and tone
  • Minimizes fine lines and open pores
  • Tights loose or crepey skin

Suitable for both men and women, microneedling works wonders on Indian skin, which is often prone to pigmentation and uneven texture.

What to Expect During the Procedure

  • Consultation: Dr. Haripriya assesses your skin concerns and suggests a tailored plan.
  • Preparation: A numbing cream is applied to make the procedure virtually painless.
  • Treatment: The microneedling pen gently creates micro-injuries, often combined with nourishing serums.
  • Aftercare: Slight redness is normal and subsides within a day or two. Sunscreen and gentle skincare are essential post-procedure.

Most patients begin to notice visible improvements after 2–3 sessions, with full results appearing over a few weeks as collagen regenerates.
