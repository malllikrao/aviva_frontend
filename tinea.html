Tinea Infections Treatment

Effective Care for Fungal Skin Infections by Dr. Samba Raju

Tinea Infections and Treatment by Dermatologist at Aviva Aesthetics, Karimnagar

By Dermatologist Dr. Samba Raju

Tinea refers to a group of common fungal infections affecting the skin, scalp, nails, and other parts of the body. Also known as ringworm, tinea infections cause red, itchy, scaly patches that can be uncomfortable and persistent if left untreated.

At **Aviva Aesthetics, Karimnagar**, our experienced dermatologist, **Dr. Samba Raju**, provides expert diagnosis and treatment to help you get relief from tinea infections and restore healthy skin.

Common Types of Tinea Infections:

  • **Tinea corporis** (body ringworm)
  • **Tinea cruris** (jock itch, groin area)
  • **Tinea pedis** (athlete’s foot)
  • **Tinea capitis** (scalp ringworm)
  • **Tinea unguium** (nail fungus)

Symptoms of Tinea Infections:

  • Circular or ring-shaped red patches
  • Itching, burning, or irritation
  • Scaling or peeling skin
  • Blisters or crusting in some cases
  • Nail thickening or discoloration (in nail infections)

Treatment Approach at Aviva Aesthetics:

Dr. Samba Raju offers customized treatment plans depending on the type and severity of infection:

  • Topical antifungal creams and ointments
  • Oral antifungal medications for extensive or resistant cases
  • Medicated shampoos for scalp infections
  • Nail care treatments for fungal nails
  • Guidance on hygiene and preventive measures to avoid recurrence

Why Choose Aviva Aesthetics for Tinea Treatment?

  • Accurate diagnosis by a skilled dermatologist
  • Use of high-quality antifungal medications
  • Personalized care for lasting results
  • Safe and hygienic clinic environment
  • Patient education for prevention and management
