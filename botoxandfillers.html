Botox: Smooth Away Lines and Wrinkles

What is Botox?

Botox (Botulinum Toxin) is a purified protein that temporarily relaxes facial muscles responsible for wrinkles and expression lines. When injected in controlled amounts, it smooths dynamic lines and prevents new ones from forming—offering a natural, wrinkle-free appearance.

Common Areas Treated with Botox:

  • Forehead lines
  • Frown lines (between the eyebrows – “11” lines)
  • Crow’s feet (lines around the eyes)
  • Bunny lines (nose wrinkles)
  • Chin dimpling
  • Neck bands
  • Brow lift (non-surgical)
  • Excessive sweating (hyperhidrosis – underarms, palms)

Why Choose Botox at Aviva Aesthetics?

  • Performed by trained medical professionals under Dr. Haripriya’s supervision
  • Use of high-quality, authentic Botox brands
  • Customized treatment plans for natural-looking results
  • Quick procedure with no downtime

We focus on subtle enhancement, not a “frozen” look—so you still look like yourself, just more refreshed.

What to Expect with Botox:

  • Duration: 10–15 minutes
  • Pain level: Minimal (tiny needle, may feel like a pinch)
  • Results: Begin within 3–5 days, full effect in 10–14 days
  • Longevity: Lasts 3 to 6 months
  • Downtime: None—you can return to normal activities right after

Is Botox Right for You?

Botox is ideal for:

  • Men and women aged 25+ looking to prevent or reduce early signs of aging
  • Those who want a non-surgical, quick solution to lines and wrinkles
  • Individuals with facial asymmetry or conditions like excessive sweating

Dermal Fillers: Restore Youth & Volume

What Are Dermal Fillers?

Dermal fillers are soft, gel-like substances—commonly made of hyaluronic acid, a natural component of our skin—that are injected beneath the skin to restore lost volume, smooth out wrinkles, and enhance facial contours.

Unlike Botox (which relaxes muscles), fillers add volume, making them ideal for areas that appear sunken, flat, or hollow with age.

Popular Areas for Filler Treatment:

  • Cheeks – for fuller, lifted contours
  • Under-eyes (tear trough) – to reduce dark circles and hollowness
  • Lips – for natural enhancement and hydration
  • Nasolabial folds – smile lines around the mouth
  • Jawline & Chin – to define facial structure
  • Marionette lines – from corners of mouth to chin

Why Choose Aviva Aesthetics for Fillers?

  • Performed by trained experts under Dr. Haripriya’s supervision
  • Use of premium, internationally approved filler brands
  • Natural-looking results with minimal pain and no downtime
  • Safe for Indian skin types, with customized treatment plans
  • Focus on facial balance, harmony, and enhancement—not overfilling

Whether you want to restore youthful volume or simply add definition to your features, our goal is to help you look refreshed, confident, and naturally beautiful.

What to Expect with Dermal Fillers:

  • Procedure time: 20–30 minutes
  • Pain level: Minimal (topical numbing cream is used)
  • Visible results: Immediately, with continued improvement over days
  • Longevity: 9 to 18 months, depending on area and product used
  • Downtime: None to minimal—mild swelling may last a day or two

Are Fillers Right for You?

Dermal fillers are ideal for:

  • Men and women aged 25+ seeking facial rejuvenation
  • Brides-to-be or individuals preparing for special occasions
  • People who want a non-surgical, quick aesthetic enhancement
  • Those concerned about early signs of aging, flat cheeks, or dull skin
