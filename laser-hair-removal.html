Laser Hair Removal in India: Everything You Need to Know

By Dr. Haripriya – Aviva Aesthetics Clinic

Unwanted body hair is a common concern among both men and women in India. From regular waxing appointments to painful threading sessions, hair removal can be time-consuming and uncomfortable. Enter laser hair removal—a modern, long-term solution that's gaining popularity across India for its convenience and effectiveness.

At Aviva Aesthetics Clinic, led by Dr.Haripriya, we offer advanced, USFDA-approved laser technology designed specifically for Indian skin tones, ensuring safe and effective results.

What is Laser Hair Removal?

Laser hair removal is a non-invasive cosmetic procedure that uses concentrated beams of light to target and destroy hair follicles. This inhibits future hair growth, resulting in smoother skin and reduced dependence on traditional methods.

At Dr.Haripriya's Aviva Aesthetics Clinic, Karimnagar we use industry-leading devices tailored for different skin and hair types to deliver precise, customized treatments.

How Does It Work?

The laser emits light that is absorbed by the pigment (melanin) in the hair. This light converts to heat and damages the follicle, inhibiting future growth. Modern technologies like Triple Diode and Nd:YAG lasers, available at Aviva Aesthetics, Karimnagar are specifically effective for Indian skin tones, minimizing risks like burns or pigmentation.

Is It Safe for Indian Skin?

Yes. Thanks to advancements in technology, laser hair removal is now safe for all skin types, including medium to darker Indian complexions. Dr.Haripriya ensures that every patient undergoes a detailed skin assessment at Aviva Aesthetics to determine the most suitable treatment plan.

How Many Sessions Are Needed?

Typically, 6 to 8 sessions spaced 4–6 weeks apart are needed. However, at Dr. Haripriya's clinic, the exact number depends on your individual hair growth cycle and area being treated. Follow-up sessions may be advised to maintain results.

Side Effects and Aftercare

Minor redness or sensitivity is normal and usually subsides within hours. At Aviva Aesthetics, we provide detailed post-treatment care instructions to ensure a smooth recovery.

Aftercare Tips from Dr. Haripriya:

  • Use sunscreen daily.
  • Avoid heat (hot showers, saunas) for 48 hours.
  • Do not pluck or wax between sessions—only shaving is recommended.
