What is Cosmetic Gynecology?

Cosmetic gynecology is a specialized branch that combines gynecology with aesthetic procedures to address concerns related to the appearance and function of female genitalia. These procedures are not just about looks—they often significantly improve comfort, sexual wellness, and self-esteem.

Our Services Include:

  • Vaginal Rejuvenation: A non-surgical or minimally invasive procedure that tightens and revitalizes the vaginal canal, often using laser or radiofrequency technology. It’s ideal for women post-childbirth or in menopause experiencing laxity or dryness.
  • Labiaplasty: This surgical procedure reduces or reshapes the labia minora or labia majora for improved comfort and appearance. It can relieve irritation and enhance self-confidence.
  • Hymenoplasty: A sensitive procedure to reconstruct the hymen, performed with complete privacy and respect.
  • Clitoral Hood Reduction: Enhances clitoral stimulation by reducing excess skin, often done in conjunction with labiaplasty for overall aesthetic enhancement.
  • G-Spot Amplification: A non-surgical procedure designed to enhance sexual pleasure by augmenting the G-spot area using fillers.
  • Platelet-Rich Plasma (PRP) Therapy: PRP injections can improve lubrication, sensitivity, and orgasmic function by promoting tissue regeneration.
