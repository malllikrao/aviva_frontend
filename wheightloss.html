Authentic Obesity & Weight Loss Management by Qualified Nutritionist at Aviva Aesthetics, Karimnagar

No Fad Diets. No Fake Powders. Just Science-Backed, Sustainable Results.

Obesity is a growing concern that affects not only appearance but also overall health. It increases the risk of diabetes, heart disease, joint pain, and more. At Aviva Aesthetics, Karimnagar, we believe in safe, natural, and sustainable weight loss — guided by our qualified nutritionist, without using artificial powders, crash diets, or unsafe shortcuts.

What Makes Our Approach Different?

Unlike trendy “quick fixes,” our program focuses on real food, real science, and real results. We do not promote fake protein shakes, weight loss powders, or extreme starvation diets.

Our Holistic Weight Loss Method Includes:

  • Personalized Diet Plans: Crafted to suit your lifestyle, preferences, and medical history — using local, seasonal, and home-style foods.
  • Scientific Nutritional Guidance: Balanced intake of macronutrients (carbohydrates, proteins, fats) and micronutrients — no cutting out essential food groups.
  • Behavioral & Lifestyle Modifications: We help you build healthier habits for long-term success — including mindful eating, improved sleep, and reduced stress.
  • Regular Monitoring & Motivation: Ongoing support, progress tracking, and meal adjustments as your body changes.
  • Exercise Guidance: Simple, practical movement routines tailored to your fitness level — no gym pressure.

What We Don’t Recommend:

  • No fake protein powders or expensive supplements
  • No crash or starvation diets
  • No false promises of “instant” weight loss
  • No harmful pills or gimmicks

Why Choose Aviva Aesthetics?

  • Qualified & Certified Nutritionist
  • Evidence-based, non-commercial approach
  • Focus on Indian food & local eating habits
  • Medical support available for comorbidities (diabetes, PCOS, etc.)
  • One-on-one support for long-term lifestyle change
