What is MNRF?

MNRF (Microneedling Radiofrequency) combines the power of microneedling with radiofrequency energy to penetrate deep into the skin layers. This dual-action technique stimulates collagen production and promotes skin remodeling, making it especially effective in treating stretch marks on areas like the abdomen, thighs, arms, and hips.

How MNRF Works on Stretch Marks:

  • Microneedles create controlled micro-injuries that trigger natural healing
  • Radiofrequency energy tightens the skin and stimulates collagen synthesis
  • Enhances skin firmness, elasticity, and tone
  • Gradually fades stretch marks and improves overall skin texture
