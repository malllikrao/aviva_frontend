Dandruff and Urticaria – Diagnosis & Treatment at Aviva Aesthetics, Karimnagar

By Dermatologist Dr. Samba Raju

Dandruff and urticaria are two common yet distinct skin and scalp conditions that can affect your comfort and confidence. At **Aviva Aesthetics, Karimnagar**, our expert dermatologist, Dr. Samba Raju, provides effective diagnosis and personalized treatment for both conditions using clinically proven methods.

What is Dandruff?

Dandruff is a scalp condition marked by flaking and itchiness, often caused by:

  • Overgrowth of yeast (Malassezia)
  • Oily or dry scalp
  • Sensitivity to hair products
  • Poor scalp hygiene
  • Weather changes

Dandruff Treatment at Aviva Aesthetics:

  • Medicated anti-dandruff shampoos (with ketoconazole, salicylic acid, etc.)
  • Scalp treatments to reduce inflammation
  • Lifestyle and dietary advice
  • Treatment of seborrheic dermatitis, if present

What is Urticaria?

Also known as hives, urticaria presents as sudden, itchy red welts on the skin. It can be:

  • Acute (lasting <6 weeks)
  • Chronic (lasting >6 weeks)

Common triggers include:

  • Allergies (food, medication, insect bites)
  • Stress
  • Temperature changes
  • Pressure or friction on skin

Urticaria Treatment at Aviva Aesthetics:

  • Antihistamines to reduce itching and inflammation
  • Allergy testing and trigger identification
  • Lifestyle and dietary modifications
  • Advanced therapies for chronic or resistant cases
  • Monitoring for associated conditions like thyroid issues or autoimmune diseases
