Say Goodbye to Pigmentation: How Chemical Peels Can Transform Your Skin

By Dr. Haripriya, Aviva Aesthetics, Karimnagar

Uneven skin tone, dark patches, and pigmentation are common concerns for many individuals across India, including in Karimnagar, where sun exposure and environmental stress play a big role. At Aviva Aesthetics, located in the heart of Karimnagar, under the expert care of Dr. Haripriya, we offer advanced chemical peel treatments that are safe, effective, and customized for Indian skin types.

What Are Chemical Peels?

Chemical peels are dermatological treatments that involve the application of a specially formulated solution to exfoliate the top layers of the skin. This stimulates the skin's natural regeneration process, helping fade pigmentation, reduce blemishes, and reveal a brighter, smoother complexion—something our clients in Karimnagar love for achieving that radiant, healthy glow.

How Do Chemical Peels Help with Pigmentation?

Pigmentation, including melasma, sunspots, and post-inflammatory hyperpigmentation, can be stubborn and hard to treat with just creams. Chemical peels work at a deeper level by:

  • Removing dead skin cells and dull surface layers
  • Targeting excess melanin responsible for dark spots
  • Boosting collagen production for healthier skin
  • Enhancing absorption of topical skincare products

This makes them an ideal choice for those in Karimnagar seeking visible results with minimal downtime.

Types of Peels We Offer at Aviva Aesthetics

At Aviva Aesthetics Karimnagar, Dr. Haripriya carefully assesses your skin type and pigmentation severity to recommend the right peel.

  • Glycolic Acid Peels

    Perfect for mild pigmentation and dull skin, helping reveal a brighter complexion.

  • Salicylic Acid Peels

    Effective for acne-prone and pigmented skin, helping clear breakouts while evening skin tone.

  • TCA Peels

    Trichloroacetic Acid peels target moderate to deep pigmentation with powerful results.

  • Lactic Acid Peels

    Ideal for sensitive and dry skin types, providing gentle yet effective exfoliation.

What to Expect After the Treatment

After a chemical peel, you may experience slight redness or peeling for a few days. It's essential to follow the post-care routine advised by Dr. Haripriya, including:

  • Strict sun protection (SPF 30 or higher)
  • Gentle moisturizing
  • Avoiding harsh products or scrubs

Most patients in Karimnagar begin to notice visible improvement within 1–3 sessions, though a series of treatments may be recommended for optimal outcomes.
