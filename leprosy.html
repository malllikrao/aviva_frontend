Leprosy Treatment

Early Diagnosis. Effective Care. Compassionate Treatment by Dr. Samba Raju

Leprosy Treatment at Aviva Aesthetics, Karimnagar

By Dermatologist Dr. Samba Raju

Leprosy, also known as Hansen’s Disease, is a chronic infectious disease caused by the bacterium Mycobacterium leprae. It primarily affects the skin, peripheral nerves, mucosa of the upper respiratory tract, and eyes. Contrary to myths, leprosy is curable, especially when detected early.

At **Aviva Aesthetics, Karimnagar**, Dr. Samba Raju, our experienced dermatologist, provides comprehensive diagnosis, treatment, and care for patients affected by leprosy with sensitivity and expertise.

Signs and Symptoms of Leprosy:

  • Light or dark skin patches with reduced sensation
  • Numbness or tingling in hands or feet
  • Muscle weakness
  • Painless ulcers on hands or feet
  • Thickened peripheral nerves
  • Eye problems in severe cases

Early treatment can prevent nerve damage, deformities, and long-term complications.

Leprosy is Not Highly Contagious

Leprosy spreads only through close, prolonged contact and is not transmitted by touch. With proper treatment, people with leprosy become non-infectious quickly.

Treatment at Aviva Aesthetics:

  • Rifampicin, Clofazimine, and Dapsone in specific combinations
  • Treatment duration: 6–12 months or longer, depending on type
  • Monitoring for nerve damage and managing complications
  • Skin care and physiotherapy advice
  • Emotional support and guidance to reduce social stigma

Why Choose Aviva Aesthetics for Leprosy Treatment?

  • Expert dermatologist care from Dr. Samba Raju
  • Use of free, WHO-supplied medications (MDT)
  • Compassionate care in a private, respectful environment

  • Patient education and counseling

Leprosy is curable, and early treatment ensures a healthy, normal life. If you or someone you know shows symptoms, consult Dr. Samba Raju at **Aviva Aesthetics, Karimnagar**, for expert care.
