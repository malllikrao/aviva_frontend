What is Rhinoplasty?

Rhinoplasty is a surgical procedure that reshapes the nose to improve appearance, function, or both. It can:

  • Refine the bridge or tip of the nose
  • Reduce or increase the nose size
  • Straighten a crooked nose
  • Correct breathing problems due to structural defects
  • Improve facial harmony

Who is Rhinoplasty For?

Rhinoplasty is suitable for:

  • Individuals unhappy with the size, shape, or proportion of their nose
  • Patients with nasal asymmetry or deformities from birth or injury
  • Those with nasal obstruction or breathing issues

Most patients are aged 18 and above, with fully developed facial features.

What to Expect from the Procedure

  • Consultation: Detailed assessment of your goals, facial structure, and medical history.
  • Surgical Plan: Dr. Saideep creates a customized surgical plan—open or closed rhinoplasty, depending on your needs.
  • Surgery: The procedure is done under anesthesia, typically taking 1.5–3 hours.
  • Recovery: Mild swelling and bruising are common for a few days. Full recovery takes a few weeks, but visible improvement starts in days.
