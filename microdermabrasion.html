Reveal Radiant Skin with Microdermabrasion at Aviva Aesthetics, Karimnagar

By Dr. Haripriya, Aviva Aesthetics, Karimnagar

Struggling with dull, tired-looking skin, fine lines, or uneven texture? Microdermabrasion is a safe, non-invasive skincare solution that’s quickly becoming a favorite in Karimnagar—and across India—for its ability to refresh and rejuvenate the skin without downtime.

At Aviva Aesthetics, led by Dr. Haripriya, we specialize in offering advanced microdermabrasion treatments tailored to Indian skin types, helping our patients achieve smoother, brighter, and healthier skin.

What is Microdermabrasion?

Microdermabrasion is a gentle exfoliating procedure that uses a specialized device to remove dead skin cells from the outermost layer of the skin. It stimulates new cell growth and improves blood circulation, making the skin look naturally youthful and vibrant.

This treatment is ideal for people in Karimnagar who face skin issues caused by sun exposure, pollution, or stress—common factors in urban and semi-urban Indian environments.

Benefits of Microdermabrasion

At our Karimnagar clinic, microdermabrasion is popular for its multiple skin benefits, including:

  • Reduction in dullness and dry patches
  • Minimizing fine lines and early wrinkles
  • Fading acne scars and mild pigmentation
  • Smoothing out rough or uneven skin texture
  • Boosting glow and overall skin health

Many clients love how their skin feels instantly smoother and looks visibly brighter after just one session.

Is Microdermabrasion Right for You?

Microdermabrasion is suitable for most skin types and tones. It is particularly effective for those dealing with:

  • Mild pigmentation
  • Age spots
  • Acne scars
  • Uneven skin tone or texture

Dr. Haripriya personally assesses each patient’s skin condition at Aviva Aesthetics, Karimnagar, to ensure the treatment is both safe and effective.

What to Expect During and After the Treatment

The treatment is quick, comfortable, and usually takes about 30–45 minutes. There’s no need for anesthesia, and most clients resume their daily routine immediately after.

Some clients may experience slight redness or mild sensitivity, which typically resolves within a few hours. Proper hydration and sun protection are recommended post-treatment.
