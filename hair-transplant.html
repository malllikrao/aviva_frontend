What is Hair Transplantation?

Hair transplantation is a minor surgical procedure in which healthy hair is taken from one part of your scalp (usually the back) and transplanted to the bald or thinning areas.

Think of it as shifting healthy plants from one part of the garden to a spot that has dried up.

Dr. Haripriya and her team at Aviva Aesthetics, Karimnagar use the latest technology and techniques to deliver natural-looking, long-lasting results.

Types of Hair Transplant Methods

There are two main types of hair transplant methods commonly used:

FUT (Follicular Unit Transplantation)

  • A strip of hair-bearing skin is removed from the back of the scalp.
  • Hair follicles are extracted and implanted in bald areas.
  • Best for covering large bald patches.

FUE (Follicular Unit Extraction)

  • Individual hair follicles are directly extracted and placed into thinning areas.
  • No visible scar, faster healing, and a more modern approach.
  • Ideal for those who want a quicker recovery.

Both options are available at Aviva Aesthetics in Karimnagar, where the approach is tailored to your specific hair pattern and goals.

Who Should Consider Hair Transplantation?

You may be a good candidate if:

  • You have male or female pattern baldness.
  • You’ve experienced hair loss due to burns or accidents.
  • You want a permanent solution to thinning hair.

Is It Painful?

Not at all. The treatment is done under local anesthesia, so you’ll be awake but won’t feel pain. There may be slight swelling or redness after the procedure, which usually fades in a few days.
