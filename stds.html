Confidential Care for Your Sexual Health by Dr. Samba Raju

STD Infections and Treatment by Dermatologist at Aviva Aesthetics, Karimnagar

By Dermatologist Dr. Samba Raju

Sexually Transmitted Diseases (STDs), also known as STIs (Sexually Transmitted Infections), are infections passed through sexual contact. They can affect both men and women and often show symptoms on the skin or genital area. Early diagnosis and treatment are important to avoid long-term health issues.

At **Aviva Aesthetics, Karimnagar**, our experienced dermatologist **Dr. Samba Raju** offers safe, confidential, and effective treatment for a wide range of STDs.

Common STD-Related Skin Conditions:

  • **Genital warts** (caused by HPV)
  • **Herpes simplex virus** (painful blisters or sores)
  • **Syphilis** (painless ulcers, rashes on palms/soles)
  • **Chancroid** (painful ulcers)
  • **Molluscum contagiosum** (small, painless bumps)
  • **Scabies and pubic lice** (itching, irritation in private areas)

Symptoms to Watch For:

  • Sores, ulcers, or rashes in genital or anal areas
  • Itching, burning, or pain while urinating
  • Unusual discharge or odor
  • Swelling or bumps on skin
  • Fever, fatigue, or body rashes (in advanced cases)

**Note:** Some STDs show no symptoms but can still spread or cause complications.

Our Treatment Approach:

Dr. Samba Raju offers a non-judgmental and confidential environment with tailored treatments including:

  • Antibiotics or antiviral medications
  • Topical creams or ointments for skin symptoms
  • Cryotherapy or electrocautery for wart removal
  • Partner treatment and counseling to prevent reinfection
  • Lifestyle and hygiene guidance to promote healing

Why Choose Aviva Aesthetics?

  • **Confidential and compassionate care**
  • Experienced dermatologist with expertise in STD-related skin issues
  • Use of high-quality, evidence-based medications
  • Emphasis on patient education, safety, and prevention
  • Discreet environment with full respect for privacy

If you notice symptoms or think you may have been exposed to an STD, seek timely medical attention. Visit **Aviva Aesthetics, Karimnagar**, and consult **Dr. Samba Raju** for safe, respectful, and professional treatment.
