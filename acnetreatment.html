Expert Acne Treatment at Aviva Aesthetics, Karimnagar

Acne is a common skin problem affecting teenagers and adults alike. It can cause pimples, blackheads, whiteheads, and sometimes painful cysts or scars. At Aviva Aesthetics, Karimnagar, our experienced dermatologist provides expert acne treatments tailored to your skin type and severity.

What Causes Acne?

Acne develops when hair follicles get clogged with oil, dead skin cells, and bacteria. Factors that can worsen acne include:

  • Hormonal changes
  • Stress
  • Poor skincare habits
  • Diet and lifestyle
  • Genetics

Our Acne Treatment Approach

Dr. Samba Raju and team offer comprehensive acne care using advanced, medically approved methods:

  • Topical creams and gels to reduce oil and inflammation
  • Oral medications including antibiotics or hormonal treatments
  • Chemical peels to unclog pores and improve skin texture
  • Laser and light therapies to target acne-causing bacteria
  • Scar treatment options to minimize marks from old acne
