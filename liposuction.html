What is Liposuction?

Liposuction is a minimally invasive surgical procedure that removes excess fat deposits from specific areas of the body. It is not a weight-loss solution but a body-shaping technique designed to enhance your natural contours and improve proportions.

Common Areas Treated:

  • Abdomen and flanks (“love handles”)
  • Thighs and hips
  • Arms and back
  • Chin and neck
  • Male chest (gynecomastia treatment)

How It Works

Using advanced liposuction techniques, our plastic surgeon gently suctions out unwanted fat through tiny incisions, ensuring minimal scarring and quick recovery. The procedure is performed under local or general anesthesia, depending on the area and amount of fat to be removed.
