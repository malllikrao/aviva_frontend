Meet Dr. Haripriya Janagama

Director, Dr.Haripriya Aviva Aesthetics

Dr. Haripriya Janagama is a highly accomplished and compassionate aesthetic physician, dedicated to helping her patients achieve their beauty and wellness goals with natural-looking results. With over 5 years of extensive experience in the field of dermatology and aesthetic medicine, she has a profound understanding of facial anatomy and skin physiology.

Her philosophy centers on a holistic approach, combining cutting-edge technology with personalized treatment plans. Dr. Haripriya is renowned for her meticulous attention to detail and her commitment to patient safety and satisfaction. She continuously updates her skills with the latest advancements in aesthetic procedures to offer the most effective and innovative solutions.

Specializations & Expertise:

  • Advanced Dermal Fillers & Injectables
  • Laser Skin Rejuvenation & Resurfacing
  • Clinical Dermatology & Acne Management
  • Hair Restoration & PRP Treatments
  • Non-Surgical Body Contouring
  • Cosmetic Gynecology

Dr. Haripriya believes in empowering her patients through education, ensuring they are fully informed about their treatment options and expected outcomes. Her warm demeanor and professional expertise make every visit to Aviva Aesthetics a comfortable and rewarding experience.
