What is Botox?

Botox (Botulinum Toxin) is a purified protein that temporarily relaxes facial muscles responsible for wrinkles and expression lines. When injected in controlled amounts, it smooths dynamic lines and prevents new ones from forming—offering a natural, wrinkle-free appearance.

Common Areas Treated with Botox:

  • Forehead lines
  • Frown lines (between the eyebrows – “11” lines)
  • Crow’s feet (lines around the eyes)
  • Bunny lines (nose wrinkles)
  • Chin dimpling
  • Neck bands
  • Brow lift (non-surgical)
  • Excessive sweating (hyperhidrosis – underarms, palms)

We focus on subtle enhancement, not a “frozen” look—so you still look like yourself, just more refreshed.

What to Expect:

  • Duration: 10–15 minutes
  • Pain level: Minimal (tiny needle, may feel like a pinch)
  • Results: Begin within 3–5 days, full effect in 10–14 days
  • Longevity: Lasts 3 to 6 months
  • Downtime: None—you can return to normal activities right after

Is Botox Right for You?

Botox is ideal for:

  • Men and women aged 25+ looking to prevent or reduce early signs of aging
  • Those who want a non-surgical, quick solution to lines and wrinkles
  • Individuals with facial asymmetry or conditions like excessive sweating
