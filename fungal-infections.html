Fungal Infections and Treatment by Dermatologist at Aviva Aesthetics, Karimnagar

By Dermatologist Dr. Samba Raju

Fungal infections are common skin problems caused by fungi that thrive in warm, moist environments. These infections can affect the skin, nails, scalp, and other body areas, causing itching, redness, scaling, and discomfort.

At Aviva Aesthetics, Karimnagar, our expert dermatologist, **Dr. Samba Raju**, offers accurate diagnosis and effective treatment plans to eliminate fungal infections and restore healthy skin.

Common Types of Fungal Infections:

  • Tinea (Ringworm) – circular, red, scaly patches
  • Athlete’s foot – itching and cracking of the feet
  • Candidiasis – yeast infection in skin folds or mouth
  • Fungal nail infections – thickened, discolored nails
  • Scalp fungal infections – flaky scalp and hair loss

Symptoms to Watch For:

  • Red, itchy, and scaly patches
  • Blisters or peeling skin
  • Nail discoloration or thickening
  • Persistent itching or burning sensation

Our Treatment Approach:

Dr. Samba Raju provides comprehensive treatment tailored to your infection type and severity:

  • Topical antifungal creams and ointments
  • Oral antifungal medications for deeper or extensive infections
  • Medicated shampoos for scalp fungal infections
  • Nail treatments including oral and topical options
  • Advice on hygiene and lifestyle to prevent recurrence

We use high-quality, **dermatologist-approved medications** and advanced therapies to ensure safe and visible results, helping you achieve lasting relief and clearer skin.
