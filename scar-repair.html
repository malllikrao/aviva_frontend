Scar Repair and Revision at Aviva Aesthetics

By Plastic Surgeon Dr. Saideep Ravuru

Scars are natural reminders of healing, but when they become prominent, uncomfortable, or aesthetically concerning, they can affect confidence and quality of life. At Aviva Aesthetics, led by renowned plastic surgeon **Dr. Saideep Ravuru**, we offer advanced scar repair and revision treatments tailored to restore both appearance and function.

Understanding Scar Revision

Scar revision is a set of procedures designed to improve the appearance of scars, making them less noticeable and more consistent with your surrounding skin tone and texture. Whether caused by surgery, trauma, acne, or burns, each scar is unique—and so is the treatment plan.

While it’s important to understand that no scar can be completely erased, the goal of scar revision is to achieve a significant cosmetic improvement and in some cases, restore mobility or function if the scar tissue is restrictive.

Common Types of Scars We Treat

At Aviva Aesthetics, Dr. Ravuru evaluates each case individually and provides treatments for:

  • **Hypertrophic Scars:** Raised scars that stay within the boundary of the original wound.
  • **Keloid Scars:** Overgrown, raised scars that extend beyond the original injury.
  • **Atrophic Scars:** Sunken or pitted scars often seen in cases of acne or chickenpox.
  • **Contracture Scars:** Typically caused by burns, these scars can restrict movement.
  • **Post-Surgical Scars:** Scars resulting from previous surgeries or procedures.

Our Advanced Treatment Options

Dr. Saideep Ravuru brings a comprehensive and customized approach to scar revision, utilizing a range of state-of-the-art modalities:

  • **Surgical Scar Revision:** Precisely excising and re-suturing scars to reduce their prominence.
  • **Laser Therapy:** Non-invasive laser treatments to smooth skin texture and reduce pigmentation.
  • **Steroid Injections:** Ideal for flattening hypertrophic and keloid scars.
  • **Dermal Fillers:** Used for lifting depressed scars to improve skin surface evenness.
  • **Microneedling and PRP:** Enhances skin regeneration and promotes collagen production.
  • **Topical Silicone Therapy:** A non-invasive option to soften and fade newer scars.
  • **Fat Grafting:** For volume restoration in atrophic or indented scars.
