Relief from Itchy, Scaly Skin

Psoriasis is a common, long-term skin condition that causes red, dry, scaly patches on the skin. It is not contagious, but it can be uncomfortable and affect your confidence.

At Aviva Aesthetics, **Dr. Samba Raju** offers safe and effective treatments to manage psoriasis, reduce flare-ups, and improve skin appearance.

Common Symptoms:

  • Red patches with white or silvery scales
  • Itching, burning, or soreness
  • Dry, cracked skin that may bleed
  • Thickened, pitted, or ridged nails
  • Swollen and stiff joints (psoriatic arthritis)
  • Most often appears on scalp, elbows, knees, or lower back

Our Treatment Approach:

  • **Topical Creams & Medicated Lotions:** First-line treatments to reduce inflammation and slow skin cell growth.
  • **Oral Medications:** For more severe cases, medications taken by mouth can target the immune response.
  • **Light Therapy (Phototherapy):** Controlled exposure to UV light helps to clear skin patches.
  • **Biologic Injections:** Advanced treatments that target specific parts of the immune system involved in psoriasis.
  • **Lifestyle & Diet Guidance:** Personalized advice to support overall skin health and minimize triggers.
  • **Customized Care:** We tailor treatment plans based on your specific type of psoriasis, its severity, and your individual needs.

We use high-quality, **dermatologist-approved medications** and advanced therapies to ensure safe and visible results, helping you achieve lasting relief and clearer skin.
