What is a Tummy Tuck?

A tummy tuck is a cosmetic surgical procedure that removes excess fat and skin from the abdomen and, in many cases, restores weakened or separated muscles. This creates a smoother, firmer abdominal profile, helping you regain confidence in your body.

Who Can Benefit?

A tummy tuck is ideal for:

  • Women with sagging skin and muscles post-pregnancy.
  • Men and women who’ve lost significant weight and are left with loose skin.
  • Individuals looking to contour their midsection for a more toned appearance.

Note: A tummy tuck is not a substitute for weight loss but a body-contouring option after you’ve reached a stable weight.

What to Expect?

  • Consultation: Your journey begins with a one-on-one consultation where your goals and medical history are discussed.
  • Surgery: Performed under anesthesia, the procedure typically lasts 2-4 hours depending on the complexity.
  • Recovery: Expect 2–4 weeks of downtime. The team at Aviva provides a detailed recovery plan and support.
  • Results: A flatter, firmer abdomen and an improved silhouette that boosts confidence.
